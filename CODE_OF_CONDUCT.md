# Code of Conduct

AgentIsland follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/). Short version:

- Be kind and respectful.
- Assume good faith.
- Disagreements about technical decisions are welcome; personal attacks and harassment are not.
- This is a small open-source project run by one person in their spare time. Patience is appreciated.

## Reporting

Email `pbokdol@gmail.com` for anything that needs handling privately. Public issues and PRs are otherwise the right place to discuss the project itself.

## Enforcement

The maintainer will respond to reports privately and act in proportion to the situation — usually a conversation, sometimes a temporary or permanent ban from the issue tracker. The Contributor Covenant text linked above lists the full impact-vs-action guidelines.
